COLORADO SWIMMING LSC

Coach Requirements

 

All coach requirements must be current and on file with the CSI Office.

View Coach Membership Requirement Checklist

1. Required Membership & Certifications

  • Non-Athlete Coach Membership
    • Register using your team’s unique link
    • Contact your Team Administrator/Registrar for access
    • Processed through USA Swimming Online Member Registration
  • CPR/AED Certification
    • Adult & Pediatric certification required
    • Valid: 2 years
    • Must be USA Swimming approved
    • Email certificate to CSI Office
  • Safety Training for Swim Coaches (STSC)
    • In-Water Certification (Lifeguard or In-Water Checklist)
    • Online Course (Red Cross)
    • Valid: 2 years
    • Submit both certificates to CSI
  • Criminal Background Check
    • Valid: 24 months
    • Completed through USA Swimming → Education → Course Catalog
    • Cost: $36 (new) / $18 (renewal)
    • Results typically within 3–5 business days
    • Not required for Junior Coaches
  • Athlete Protection Training (APT)
    • Required annually
    • Complete via USA Swimming Education → Course Catalog
    • Must finish quiz and evaluation
  • USADA Coach Advantage Tutorial (CAT)
    • Required annually
    • Same access as APT
    • Automatically links to your record
  • Concussion Training (CPT)
    • Required annually
    • Required by USA Swimming and Colorado law
    • Certificate must be emailed to CSI Office
    • Options:
      • NFHS Course
      • CDC Heads Up Course
  • USA Swimming App
    • Used to verify membership and certifications
    • Serves as your electronic membership card at meets

2. Coach Education Requirements

  • Existing Coaches (Completed FOC 101 & 201 before 9/1/2022)
    • Complete Quality Coaching Framework (QCF)
  • New Coaches
    • Before being on deck:
      • Welcome to USA Swimming
      • Quality Coaching Framework (QCF)
    • Within first 365 days:
      • Core Certification (5 modules over 1 year)
      • Rules & Regulations for Coaches

3. Athlete Protection Training (APT) Details

  • Who must complete:
    • All coaches
    • All non-athlete members
    • Adult athletes (18+)
  • Course Progression:
    • SafeSport Trained Core
    • Refresher 1 → Refresher 2 → Refresher 3
  • Important:
    • Do NOT take “SafeSport for Adult Athletes” (does not count)
  • How to Access:
    • Log into SWIMS
    • Go to Education → Course Catalog
    • Select correct course based on progression

4. Submitting Certificates

  • Required to email to CSI Office:
    • CPR/AED
    • STSC (In-Water & Online)
    • Concussion Training
  • Send to: csiswimoffice@swimcolorado.org
  • Tip: Even if a course should auto-sync, send your certificate to be safe.

5. CPR / STSC Course Locations

  • Fort Collins Parks & Recreation
  • Loveland Parks & Recreation
  • Carbon Valley Parks & Recreation
  • Denver Parks & Recreation
  • Safety Solved (Aurora)
  • Colorado Springs Parks & Recreation
  • Grand Junction Parks & Recreation

6. Tips & Troubleshooting

  • Use Chrome or Firefox (updated versions)
  • Disable pop-up blockers
  • Complete ALL steps in each course (including closing screens)
  • Take a screenshot of completion (with date/time)
  • If courses do not appear or update:
    • Submit a Help Desk ticket in SWIMS
    • Include name, member ID, and screenshots

7. Downloading Certificates (SafeSport)

  • Go to safesporttrained.org
  • Login or reset password
  • Access Menu → Transcript
  • Download certificate
