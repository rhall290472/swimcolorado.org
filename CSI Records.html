OLDEST CSI LCM RECORDS

 

Womens LCM CSI Record
    Oldest womens's record: Elizabeth Knorre (CSST-CO / Colorado Springs Swim Team), 100 Free, 1:05.93, set 1-Jan-88
  • Elizabeth went on to swim for the Colorado State University Rams and swam in 4 events in the 1998 US Nationals.

 

Mens SCY LCM Record
    Oldest Men;s record: George DiCarlo (CRST-CO), 800 Free, 8:3.92, set 1-Jan-81
  • George went on to swim the 400m and 1500m Freestyle in the 1984 Los Angeles Olympics. He won the 400m Freestyle with a time of 3:51.24, breaking the Olympic record, and places 2nd in the 1500 with a time of 15:10.59.
