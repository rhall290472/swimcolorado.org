Age Group Development Committee - The members of the Age Group Development Committee shall consist of the Age Group Vice Chair, 14&Under Age Group Coaches representing diverse areas of the state, and a sufficient number of athletes appointed so as to constitute at least twenty percent (20%) of the voting membership of the Committee. The Age Group Committee shall seek knowledge from current 14&Under athletes who shall serve on the committee with voice and no vote. The Age Group Vice Chair or his/her designee will be the chair of this committee.
