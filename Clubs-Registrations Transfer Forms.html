COLORADO SWIMMING LSC

Registrations and Transfers

All Transfers and registrations are being iniated by the parent in the new SWIMS 3.0.

Fees 2026 Registration Year:

  • Premium Athlete Fee = USA Swimming fee $70 + LSC (Colorado) Fee $15 = $85
  • If add Junior Coach = additional $15
  • Outreach Athlete Fee = USA Swimming fee $5 + LSC (Colorado) Fee $0 = $5 (must provide club with required verification Free/Reduced letter from school district, CHP+, or Medicaid)
  • Flex Athlete Fee = USA Swimming fee $20 + LSC (Colorado) Fee $10 = $30
  • (Flex is only available for 12 & under swimmers and up to 2 swim meets)
  • Seasonal Athlete Fee (April 1st-August 29th) = USA Swimming fee $40 + LSC (Colorado) Fee $7 = $47
  • Non-Athlete Fee (Coach, Official, Other) = USA Swimming fee $70 + LSC (Colorado) Fee $15 = $85
  • Non-Athlete Fee (Administrator absoluetly no athlete contact) = USA Swimming fee $30 + LSC (Colorado) Fee $0 = $30

Transfer Fees:

  • Billed to the club after the transfer has been iniated
  • $10 transfer fee 
    • Exceptions include (not currently registered, college affliation, moved from out of LSC, team dissovled)

If you are truly unattached and not affilated with a club, please Contact for an unattached registration link.

For the most up-to-date policy, please refer to the CSI Policy and Procedures Manual
