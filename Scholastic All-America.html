SCHOLASTIC ALL-AMERICA

APPLICATION DETAILS

Application Opens on June 1, 2024.

The applicant must have all application documents completed by September 6, 2024. Late applications will NOT be accepted or considered.

The applicants will be required to have a complete transcript for the 2023-2024 academic year available for uploading to the application. Report/grade cards will not be accepted.

The applicant must have been a member in good standing of USA Swimming at the time of the swim and a current member when applying for the 2023-2024 SAA team.
