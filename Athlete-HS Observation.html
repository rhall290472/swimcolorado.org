COLORADO SWIMMING

High School Swim Observation

CSI athlete members may request that their times be entered into the USA Swimming SWIMS database for observed meets. Athletes must complete the appropriate form below for Girls or Boys high school swims.

 

Girls HS Swims Boys HS Swims

IMPORTANT DEADLINE

Athletes must sign up before the meet ends.

No late sign-ups will be accepted.

  • Required by CSI Policies & Procedures
  • Times will not count if missed
WHO NEEDS TO SIGN UP?
  • USA Swimming athletes
  • Competing in a high school Observed meet
  • Wanting times entered into the SWIMS database
WHAT IS OBSERVATION?

Officials verify the meet follows USA Swimming rules so times can be officially recognized.

IMPORTANT REMINDERS
  • Do not wait until you arrive
  • WiFi may be limited
  • You can sign up before the meet
  • No exceptions after the meet ends
