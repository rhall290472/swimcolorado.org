Hale Adams and Janet Kralik Memorial Scholarship

Opens: 4/1/2026 – Closed: 6/1/2026

About the Scholarship
The Hale Adams and Janet Kralik Memorial Scholarship provides financial assistance to high school seniors who are members of USA Swimming in Colorado and planning to attend college in any state.
· Essay Required: Yes
· Need-Based: No
· Merit-Based: Yes
· Win $1,500

 

Requirements
This program is open to seniors graduating from high schools in Colorado who are registered athlete members of USA Swimming.
· Applicants must be planning to enroll at a college or university in any state.
· They must have a GPA (weighted) of 3.5 or higher.
· United States Citizen. Girls and boys are judged separately.

Hale Adams (Eugene “Hale” Adams, Jr.) and Janet Kralik were longtime volunteers and contributors to Colorado Swimming. A memorial scholarship is named after both of them.

 

Hale Adams
Full name: Eugene “Hale” Adams, Jr. (born June 16, 1941, in Denver, CO - February 13, 2005 in Grand Junction, CO)

 

He was a dedicated volunteer in the swimming community. His involvement started in Grand Junction and expanded to significant roles in Colorado Swimming and USA Swimming programs. He was known for his love of life, sense of humor, and extensive volunteer work.

 

The Hale Adams Memorial Fund/Scholarship was established in his memory to support Colorado youth swimming.
Janet Kralik
Full name: Janet Marie Kralik (née Gordon; November 21, 1948 – May 5, 2017).

 

She was a lifelong Pueblo, Colorado resident. She worked for Colorado Swimming for 22 years as Executive Secretary, where she was a strong advocate and support for swimmers, touching many lives.

 

She was actively involved in administrative and volunteer roles (e.g., board meetings) for the organization.
