SCHOLASTIC ALL-AMERICA

APPLICATION DETAILS

 

Each year, USA Swimming recognizes its members who excel both in the classroom and in the pool. The Scholastic All-America Team is made up of high school student-athletes who have a grade point average of 3.5 or higher and who have achieved the required time standard in a single event.

 

The applicants will be required to have a complete transcript for the academic year available for uploading to the application. Report/grade cards will not be accepted.

 

The applicant must have been a member in good standing of USA Swimming at the time of the swim and a current member when applying for the SAA team.
