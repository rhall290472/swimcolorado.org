USA Swimming encourages people with disabilities to participate in the sport of swimming and facilitates their inclusion in USA Swimming Programs through education and collaboration.

We seek to involve people with disabilities in existing competitions and programs for all swimmers, rather than provide unique disability-only opportunities.  Swimmers who prefer disability-only programs should consult the links menu in the "Get Involved" Section.

    May 9-11, 2025 - 22nd Annual Bill Keating Jr. Memorial Cincinnati Para-Swimming Open

     

    Cincinnati, OH – Get ready for an incredible weekend of competition, and camaraderie, at the 22nd Annual Bill Keating Jr. Memorial Cincinnati Para-Swimming Open! This highly anticipated event will take place May 9-11, 2025, at Keating Natatorium (616 W. North Bend Rd., Cincinnati, OH).

    This meet is more than just a competition—it's an opportunity to support and celebrate swimmers with disabilities as they advance in their journey to U.S. Paralympics Swimming. As one of just three Para Open Competitions , it offers national certification opportunities and vital development experiences for athletes, coaches, and officials alike.
